Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does Subs track my subscriptions?

Subs allows you to manually add and manage your subscriptions. Simply input the details of each subscription and Subs will track renewal dates, send reminders, and analyze your spending.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never store your actual payment details. Your subscription data is stored securely and we never share it with third parties.

Can I export my subscription data?

Yes, you can export all your subscription data in various formats including CSV and JSON for your records or analysis.

Can I cancel my subscription to Subs?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. We'll even help you download your data before you go.
